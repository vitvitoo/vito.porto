Surabaya, Indonesia UI/UX · Digital Marketing · Event Ops

I got my start not behind a design tool, but behind a clipboard — coordinating logistics, sponsors, and promo timelines for school events. That's where I learned the thing most designers only learn later: a beautiful interface means nothing if nobody shows up to use it.

Today I split my focus between UI/UX design — turning messy ideas into interfaces people move through without thinking — and digital marketing, where I plan the campaigns that get those interfaces in front of the right people.

It's an unusual combination, but it means I design things that are meant to be found, not just admired.
